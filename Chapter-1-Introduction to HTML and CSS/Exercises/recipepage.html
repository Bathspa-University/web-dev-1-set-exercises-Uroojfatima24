The Ultimate Fudgy Brownie Recipe

A classic recipe for chewy, chocolatey, and fudgy brownies.

SERVINGS: 9 | PREP TIME: 15 MINS | COOKING TIME: 25 MINS

Ingredients:

  • Chocolate 220g
  • Butter 3/4 cup
  • Sugar 1 1/4 cups
  • Eggs 2
  • Vanilla 2 teaspoons
  • All-purpose flour 3/4 cup
  • Cocoa powder 1/4 cup
  • Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Chop the chocolate into chunks. Melt half, save half.
  3. Combine the melted butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla. Beat for 1-2 minutes until the blend is fluffy and light-colored.
  4. Whisk the melted chocolate into the blend.
  5. Strain the flour, cocoa powder, and salt into the bowl. Fold the ingredients together, being careful not to overmix.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chunks saved earlier, then transfer the batter into the parchment paper-lined dish.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Then, cool completely.
  8. Slice and Serve.
